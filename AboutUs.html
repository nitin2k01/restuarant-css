About Us
A restaurant is a business (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Business) that prepares and serves food (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Food) and drinks (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Drink) to customers (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Customer).[1] (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Restaurant#cite_note-1) Meals are generally served and eaten on the premises (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Premises), but many restaurants also offer take-out (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Take-out) and food delivery services (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Delivery_(commerce)). Restaurants vary greatly in appearance and offerings, including a wide variety of cuisines (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cuisine) and service (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Customer_service) models ranging from inexpensive fast-food restaurants (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast-food_restaurant) and cafeterias (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cafeteria) to mid-priced family restaurants (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Family_restaurant), to high-priced luxury establishments Restaurants are classified or distinguished in many different ways. The primary factors are usually the food itself (e.g. vegetarian (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Vegetarianism), seafood (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seafood), steak (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Steak)); the cuisine (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cuisine) (e.g. Italian, Korean, Chinese, Japanese, Indian, French, Mexican, Thai) or the style of offering (e.g. tapas (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tapas) bar, a sushi (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Sushi) train, a tastet restaurant, a buffet (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Buffet) restaurant or a yum cha (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Yum_cha) restaurant). Beyond this, restaurants may differentiate themselves on factors including speed (see fast food (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast_food)), formality, location, cost, service, or novelty themes (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Theme_restaurant) (such as automated restaurants (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Automated_restaurant)). Some of these also include fine dining (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fine_dining), casual dining, contemporary casual, family style, fast casual, fast food (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Fast_food), cafes (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Coffeehouse), buffet, concession stands, food trucks, pop-up restaurants, diners (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Diner), and ghost restaurants (https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ghost_restaurant).
